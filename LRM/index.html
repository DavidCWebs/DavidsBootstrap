About Me

I was born and raised in Shannon Town and now live in Stonehall with my husband Ger and three sons Ged, Jamie and Darren.

I work as an accounts administrator with a local company. I have a keen interest in sports and I am currently the Vice Treasurer of Shannon Town Utd AFC.


My Priorities

I promise to bring YOUR voice to the County Council. My priorities are:

  • Upgrade Shannon water treatment plant and sewage system
  • More Gardai patrolling our streets and industrial estates
  • Maintaining assistance from the EU so the family farms in Clare continue to survive
  • Acceptable teacher/pupil ratios and special needs provision in all schools
  • To ensure the continued promotion of Shannon Airport
  • To support a pro-business approach with fair rates and compliance regulations
